Do’s & Don’ts

Respect the National Flag

Do’s

  • Hoist the flag at sunrise, lower it before sunset.
  • Always keep the saffron at the top.

Don’ts

  • Do not let the flag touch the ground.
  • Never use the flag for decoration or commercial purposes.
