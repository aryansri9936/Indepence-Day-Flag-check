Guidelines for Using the National Flag

  • The flag should always occupy the position of honor.
  • No other flag should be placed higher or above the Indian Flag.
  • The saffron band should always be on top when hoisted horizontally.
  • The flag should never touch the ground or water.
  • It must not be used as a drapery, costume, or decoration.
