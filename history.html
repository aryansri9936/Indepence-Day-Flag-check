History of the Tiranga

The evolution of India’s National Flag

Timeline

1906: The first unofficial flag of India was hoisted in Calcutta.

1921: Pingali Venkayya presented a version with two colors and a spinning wheel.

1931: The tricolor with saffron, white, and green was adopted with the spinning wheel.

1947: The current Tiranga with the Ashoka Chakra was officially adopted on July 22.
